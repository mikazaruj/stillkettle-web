Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. Stella uses end-to-end encryption for all data. We never store your invoice content on our servers—only the extracted metadata needed for logging. Your Gmail connection is secured through OAuth 2.0.

Does it work with invoices in other languages?

Absolutely. Stella's AI recognizes invoices in English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, and many more languages. It also handles different number formats and currency symbols.

Can I use it with spreadsheet apps other than Google Sheets?

Yes. Stella works with Google Sheets, Microsoft Excel, and any spreadsheet app that supports Google Sheets API integration.
