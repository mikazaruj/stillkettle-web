Questions?

Will Stella scan ALL my emails?

No. You tell Stella which vendors to watch. It only processes those emails. Your inbox stays private.

What happens to my data?

It goes straight to your Google Sheet. We don't store it. Your data stays yours.

Does it work in different languages?

Yes. Our AI reads invoices in any language -- German, French, Spanish, Arabic, and more.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel instantly. Your data stays in your Google Sheet.
