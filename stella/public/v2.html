Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. Stella uses OAuth 2.0 to connect to Gmail. We don't store invoice content -- only extracted fields (vendor, amount, date) pass through our servers on the way to your sheet.

Does it work with invoices in other languages?

Yes. Stella reads invoices in English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, and more. It also recognizes different number formats and currency symbols.

Can I use it with spreadsheet apps other than Google Sheets?

Yes. Stella works with Google Sheets, Microsoft Excel, and any spreadsheet app that supports Google Sheets API integration.

What if I need to cancel my Pro subscription?

Cancel anytime. No contracts, no questions. Your data stays in your Google Sheet -- you just lose Pro features like automated scheduling.

How does the Pro free trial work?

7 days of Pro features, completely free. No credit card needed. If you don't upgrade by day 7, your account reverts to the Free plan automatically.
