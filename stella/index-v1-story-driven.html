Questions?

No. You tell Stella which vendors to watch. It only processes those emails. Your inbox stays private.

What happens to my data?

It goes straight to your Google Sheet. We never store it. Your data stays yours.

Does it work in different languages?

Yes. Our AI extracts data regardless of invoice language. German, French, Spanish, Arabic, more.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel instantly. Your data stays in your Google Sheet.
