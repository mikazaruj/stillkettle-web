FAQ

Common questions

Is my email data safe?

Yes. Stella only reads emails that contain invoices. We never store your email content on our servers. All processing happens in-browser via the Chrome extension. Your data stays on your device.

What email providers are supported?

Currently Gmail only, via the Chrome extension. Outlook support is on our roadmap and coming soon.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel your Pro plan anytime and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period. The free plan never expires.

What invoice formats are supported?

Stella handles PDF attachments, inline HTML invoices, and image-based invoices in any language. Our AI improves with every scan.

How accurate is the data extraction?

Stella achieves 98% accuracy on structured invoices. You can always review and edit extracted data before exporting. The AI learns from corrections to improve over time.
